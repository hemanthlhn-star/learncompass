Built by a learner,
for every learner.

Hi, I'm Hemanth Naidu Latchireddi — and I built LearnCompass because I was once that student staring at a blank screen, not knowing where to start.

I spent months jumping between random YouTube videos, incomplete courses and conflicting advice. There was no single place that said: here is exactly what to learn, in what order, with what resources, and how to practice.

So I built it. LearnCompass is the guide I wish I had when I started. Every roadmap, every resource link, every project idea and interview question is hand-picked and battle-tested.

"My mission is simple — no student should ever feel lost about where to start or what to learn next. Knowledge should be structured, accessible and free."
— Hemanth Naidu Latchireddi, Founder
